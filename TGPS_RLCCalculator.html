«Process of Getting RL Custom:»
  • First you must have an approved quest thread in the guild. Alternatively, if you wish to details to remain secret from others, you may PM the mule, The Gourmet Pocky Shoppe, or Mystaira on devART, with your quest details and have that approved.
  • Once RL custom slots are open and you have applied for a slot (via Gaia or DA), if chosen I will reply letting you know and provide you with my Paypal email.
  • You send the payment via Paypal to my Paypal email.
      • Please attach a note with your Gaia username (and DA name if applying through DA) and also the flavor with the payment so I know who sent it.
      • For ease, choose "Other" under "Personal" payments.
  • Once payment is received, I will create the pocky stage and post the cert in the thread.
«Rules & Info for RL Customs:»
  • Send the form to the mule, The Gourmet Pocky Shoppe, on Gaia or to Mystaira on devART.
  • If the pocky is a gift for someone, please provide their username as well.
  • Just a caution, but the pocky boy stage may take an indeterminate amount of time to be done due to inspiration and other workloads.
  • All child and bishie stages are fullbody.
« Terms of Use for RL Customs »
  • You may use the commissioned Pocky artwork for personal use only.
  • You may NOT resell or use the commissioned artwork for commercial purposes without discussing it with me first.
  • You may NOT claim the artwork as your own and will issue credit as necessary. And please do not remove signature without asking.
  • As the artist, I have the right to display the artwork on DA or other sites.
