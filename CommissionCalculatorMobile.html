« Terms of Use for Commissions »
➤ You may use the commissioned artwork for personal use only.
➤ You may NOT resell or use the commissioned artwork for commercial purposes without discussing it with me first. And if resale (adoptables/designs only) is approved, you may NOT sell it for more than you paid for it.
➤ You may NOT claim the artwork as your own and will issue credit as necessary. And please do not remove my signature without asking.
➤ As the artist, I have the right to display the artwork on DA or other sites. (I will however respect a commissioner's request to not display their commission on specific sites or at all online.)
«Process of Commissioning:»
1 • Send me a message with 'Commission' in the subject, with the commission form filled out.
2 • After looking it over and approving it, I will reply letting you know and provide you with my Paypal email. (Unless payment is in points.)
3 • You must send payment via Paypal (or DA for points) to my Paypal email. (please attach a note with your deviantArt (or wherever) username, and a brief summary of your commission(i.e. black haired demon man)). Please use the "goods and services" option and don't forget to select "no shipping required".
4 • I send you the final product. (with updates in between upon request)
«Rules for Commissions:»
➤ No deadlines please. I will try to finish your commission within a reasonable amount of time, but other workloads and lack of inspiration may delay the final product. You may ask if I could accept a deadline, but I cannot guarantee the quality of the final product if the deadline is too unreasonable.
➤ Once your commission is approved, you must send at least half (or all) of the payment upfront before I will start sketching. The final product will not be delivered until payment has been delivered in full.
➤ Upon request, I will provide updates of your commission. (watermarked if full payment hasn't been received yet.)
➤ If full payment was not received upfront, then when I finish I will send you a watermarked version of the finished piece for approval. After receiving full payment, I will send the final art without the watermark.
➤ I can send higher resolution images via email if requested (as I can only upload so big without it getting resized).
«Other Information & Disclaimer:»
➤ I'm still learning and am better at drawing some things than others. Check my gallery
➤ My art is all completely digital and created predominately in Paint Tool SAI with Wacom Intuos5 tablet.
➤ If you do not have a deviantART account, you may contact me however you are able to. (ex. PM me on a forum (i.e. Gaia or Solia), another art site I am on (i.e. Toyhouse, Art Station, Artician), any social site (i.e. Facebook, Tumblr, Twitter), or you may email me if you already have my email. I may or may not respond to PMs from other sites since I may not be active on them.)
➤ I'm always willing to try drawing new things, so feel free to ask if I am up to drawing something if there isn't any examples of it in my gallery.
➤ I am open to nude/erotic/ecchi/yaoi commissions. Though I ask for your requests to be tasteful. Any depiction of hardcore sexual content, hentai, or anything generally "inappropriate" will for the most part be denied immediately, no exceptions. If you're confused, feel free to ask me and I'll let you know.
